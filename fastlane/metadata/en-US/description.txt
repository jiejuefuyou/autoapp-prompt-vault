The prompt manager that doesn't sync to a server.

Save the AI prompts you actually use — for ChatGPT, Claude, Midjourney, ComfyUI, Coze, anything. Type your placeholders as {{variables}}. When you need a prompt, tap it, fill the blanks, copy. Done.

WHY THIS APP EXISTS
Most prompt managers want to be a marketplace, a team workspace, or a subscription. PromptVault is a single-screen iPhone app that does one thing: keep your prompts findable and reusable. No account. No cloud. No upsell.

WHAT IT DOES
• Save prompts with title, body, and tags
• Use {{variable}} placeholders — fill them when you need to copy
• Live preview of the rendered prompt before you copy
• Search across titles, bodies, and tags
• One-tap copy with usage stats per prompt
• Pull-out tag filter so big libraries stay navigable

WHAT IT DOESN'T DO
• No accounts. No login.
• No network calls. Zero. Verifiable in the source repo.
• No analytics or third-party SDKs
• No cloud sync — your prompts stay on your device
